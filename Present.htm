Present

I received the Master's Degree in Library Science in 1988 and started my career as  Librarian in 1988 in a private organization's Public library with part time tutoring at school level. Meanwhile I got enrolled for PhD degree in the faculty of Languages (Hindi) in 1988.  After serving the public library for approximately one years , I had a chance to Visit Charles University, Prague, The then Czechoslovakia in late 1990 where I submitted eight research papers on "Indian Knowledge preservation techniques" and had a chance to Visit Bodleian Library, Oxford, UK where attended two of the inter-active courses on Information Technology. After attaining my PhD Degree In Languages (Hindi) from Panjab University, Chandigarh in 1994 I  started serving the University Level at Assistant  Librarian which continued up to 2005.

Presently I am working as Associate Professor in the Department of VVBIS & IS of Panjab University, Chandigarh, India at Sadhu Ashram Hoshiarpur. Punjab. India since 2005. I am engaged in teaching at specialized Graduate classes in Sanskrit and Doctorate in Hindi literature. So far six  students have attained their Doctorate degree under my supervision.

My research interest encompasses  the field of Homeopathy, Ayurveda, Psychology, Language Studies, Indology, Sanskrit Studies, and alike fields.
